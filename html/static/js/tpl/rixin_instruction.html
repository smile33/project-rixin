Return/Replace Instructions

All products from RIXIN, on which indicated manufacturer name and part number are exact , please check part number, brand and other related information carefully before you place order. RIXIN will not accept any returns or replacements requests if customers make mistake by themselves.

1.Acceptance conditions for returns or replacements:

1). Please make sure the receipt and check of items within one week after receipt of the goods.

2). If there are quality problems, please show the test report from your authority or submitted use detected feedback report within 30 days, it shall not be accepted once expired. All products those need to return or replace, please be sure to retain the original packaging and label.

3). If found the actual number of receipt and the model do not match, Please provide the receipt of the photos and related pictures to prove, once confirm that RiXin or suppliers sent the wrong goods , RiXin will arrange the replacements timely for you.

4). If apply for returns because of product quality problems, you need to provide detailed performance test report ,which act as replacements or returns proofs for RiXin to negotiate with suppliers.

5). About purchase order issued VAT invoice, you need to also send back to us after returns confirmed.

2.Non-acceptance conditions for returns or replacements

1). Original packaging and labels are damaged or missing.

2). Already used or tested products (with the exception of product quality problems)

3).If the customer within 30 days without any objection to the quality of the product, the default product quality is fully in line with the requirements.

4). The customer's own mistake for the wrong orders.

3. Return address : if returns or replacements confirmed, we will offer you concrete shipment address for returns or replacements.

4. Process time for customer service:

1). RiXin customer service will accept and solve in 2 days after customers submit returns applications.

2). RiXin will transfer refund or replace for goods after getting back returns

5.Returns freight Description:

1). Because of the wrong quantities, the wrong model, the product quality problems caused returns, RiXin will bear the freight

2). As a result of your mistake led to returns, Rixin will not bear the freight
